We are testing the Linear Representation Hypothesis for sentiment in Gemma 2 2B's layer 12.
The linear probe finds the single direction in the 2304-dim residual stream that best separates positive from negative reviews — 
its weight vector is the candidate sentiment direction. If this direction achieves high accuracy on held-out test data, it confirms 
that sentiment is represented linearly at this layer. The SAE will independently produce its own candidate sentiment direction without
seeing any labels, and the cosine similarity between the two will tell us how much the methods agree.
